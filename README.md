Developed an interactive Flipkart Grocery Analytics Dashboard using Power BI and Excel to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and store-level insights across multiple retail categories.

🔹 Key Highlights:
Analyzed 8,500+ orders with total sales exceeding 1.2M
Designed KPI cards for Total Sales, Total Orders, Average Sales, and Average Rating
Built dynamic visualizations for Sales by Category, City Tier, and Shop Type
Tracked shop opening trends from 2021–2024
Implemented slicers for year, city tier, and shop size to enable interactive filtering

🔹 DAX Measures Used:
Total Sales = SUM(Sales)
Total Orders = COUNT(Item Code)
Avg Sales = AVERAGE(Sales)
Avg Rating = AVERAGE(Rating)
Sales by Tier = CALCULATE(SUM(Sales), ALLEXCEPT(Data, City Tier))
Year-wise Sales = CALCULATE(SUM(Sales), YEAR(Date))

🔹 Tools & Techniques:
Power BI (DAX, Data Modeling, Interactive Dashboards)
Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning & Preprocessing)
Data Visualization & KPI Tracking

🔹 Insights Delivered:
Tier 3 cities contributed the highest revenue share
Supermarket Type 1 had the maximum sales and order volume
Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods emerged as top-performing categories
Sales showed a declining trend in newly opened shops after 2023
